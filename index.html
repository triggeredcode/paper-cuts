The Idea

Take a unit square. Make a few straight cuts. How many regions did you just create? The answer involves planar straight-line graphs and a forest of design choices most tutorials skip.

The essay names the problem and surfaces the questions. The game lets you trip over them yourself. Either order works — the playground is forgiving, the essay is opinionated.
